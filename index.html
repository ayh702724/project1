In the 2000s, Black Series appears




The mid-2000s marked a major turning point in the history of Mercedes-Benz and AMG, with the introduction of the very first AMG Black Series model in 2006. These high-performance machines are the epitome of excellence, and to date, only six models have ever been created, making them truly rare and coveted.


The first Black Series AMG was born from the Mercedes-Benz SLK 55 AMG.


initially serving as a Safety Car in 2004 and 2005 before being released as a road car in 2006.


Despite its compact size, the SLK 55 AMG Black Series boasted a mighty 5.5-liter V8 engine that generated 400bhp.


propelling the car from 0 to 62mph in a mere 4.5 seconds.
